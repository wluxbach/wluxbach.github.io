What is Procrastination?

Procrastinate (verb)

pro·cras·ti·nate

Procrastination is "delaying or putting off an activity." Procrastination is a habit that can lead to stress, anxiety, and depression. Procrastination connects to laziness, poor time management, or the thought that the pressure of a closer deadline leads to better work.
