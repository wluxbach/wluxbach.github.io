Who are we?

We are a group of high schoolers from Catalina Foothills High School who created this site as a computer science project. We also have personal experience with procrastination and would like to share our methods of overcoming it.

JulieTFarbarik, CC BY-SA 4.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Why do we care about your procrastination?

We don't want people to procrastinate on school work or work in the real world. We want to tell people about procrastination and help them learn how to prevent it.
